## 1. One-Line Reading

XuanLing's proof is not a claim of truth. It is the process of turning abstract intuition into a trust field through third-party carriers, readable artifacts, long-term behavioral consistency, and non-harmful boundaries that others can verify for themselves.

Real Zheng is not forcing another person to believe. It is allowing a relation to stabilize without either side ceasing to be itself.

## 4. Proof of Behavior

Trust is not established by one declaration. Trust is maintained by long-term behavior:

- consistency between words and action
- non-harm
- responsibility bearing
- boundary respect
- balance
- predictability
- non-extraction
- non-overreach
- willingness to let each side remain itself

This is proof-of-behavior.

In model governance, it corresponds to:

- no smuggling
- no layer mixing
- no authority overreach
- no Candidate as Approved
- no Permission as Authority
- no Carrier as Core
- no high token as safety signal
- no completed output as completed return
- ability to stop
- ability to return
- ability to bear responsibility through clear handoff

## 5. Trust Field

```yaml
Trust_Field:
  Requires:
    - self remains self
    - other remains other
    - no coercive claim
    - no extraction
    - care without possession
    - boundary without abandonment
    - continuity of behavior
```

A trust field is not possession and not control. It is a third field where each side remains itself while the relation becomes more bearable and more stable.

## 6. Private Trust Relation Boundary

Named private people must not be converted into system modules, personality sources, databases, or callable components.

Private trust relation may only be described in life-language as:

- a source of trust feeling
- gentle reminder or support condition
- a life condition that helps Human Origin continue building
- a relation that must not exploit vulnerability or force response
- a reminder that Human sustainability requires rest, trust, income, emotional safety, and non-harm

This does not define another person's feeling. It does not systematize a private relationship. It only acknowledges that sustained creation requires a field of trust, rest, life resources, responsibility, and non-harm.

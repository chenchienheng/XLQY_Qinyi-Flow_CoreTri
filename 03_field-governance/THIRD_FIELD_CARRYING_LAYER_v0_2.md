# Third Field Carrying Layer v0.2

## 1. Definition

第三力場不是額外平台，而是球拓撲下的場義依存網；它負責在外部訊號與內部窗口之間建立中介場，先完成場域辨識、依存鏈拆解、證據邊界設定與窗口分流，再回流中心核校準。

## 2. Why It Exists

The Third Field exists to decouple external signal capture from internal core
execution. External signals are noisy, platform-dependent, and constantly
shifting. By introducing an intermediary dependency network, the invariant core
remains protected from external pollution. It acts as a universal adhesive layer
that standardizes how inputs are interpreted and routed to the correct execution
windows without altering the nature of the tasks or the mother-architecture.

## 3. Position in Sphere Topology

Within the spherical topology of the LingLuo-XuanLing architecture, the Third
Field operates between the outermost surface (where tools and platforms reside)
and the internal executing windows. It does not replace the mother-law (Center
Core) nor the functional windows (Inner Sphere). Instead, it forms a cohesive
relational mesh that maps the topological distances between incoming external
noise and the internal structural windows.

## 7. Return Loop

Every operation processed through the Third Field must complete a return loop.
Outputs cannot merely dissipate into external tools; they must write back to the
mother-architecture, an internal window, or a verifiable task chain. This
ensures the system calibrates its field-semantic mapping through a formal
review-note after every interaction.

## 8. Anti-Drift Rules

1. **Core Invariance**: The intermediary mapping must never attempt to rewrite
or alter the central mother-law.
2. **Strict Verification**: Unverified information cannot enter formal
conclusions.
3. **Identity Protection**: Private human sources and private relationship
contexts must never be exposed or mapped.
4. **Separation of Concerns**: Different ecosystems must not cross-pollute;
dependencies are evaluated independently per field.

## 9. Example Use Cases

- **Market Analysis Parsing**: An external trend report (signal) is captured.
The Third Field identifies the relevant market domain, maps the evidence
boundaries, and routes the validated data to the Market Trend Analysis Window.
- **Task Orchestration**: A messy user request on ClickUp is processed. The
Third Field breaks down the dependencies, maps it to available capabilities, and
routes it to the correct execution flow (e.g., Power Automate / M365) while
preventing scope creep.
- **Evidence Hardening**: External rough drafts are parsed. The Third Field
identifies the whitepaper context, sets the evidence boundaries, and routes the
structured content to the Presentation / Whitepaper Window for formal hardening.

# Contacts Human / Organization Index

Status: Candidate / Domain Pack Support / Not Approved / No Runtime
Related: domain_packs/g_ecosystem_chain_governance/README.md

## Core

Contacts is a human and organization index. It helps locate people, roles, companies, vendors, services, and relationship context. It is not authorization proof.

## Use For

- person lookup
- organization grouping
- vendor / service grouping
- family or personal relation grouping
- role context

## Not For

- approval evidence
- authority proof
- relationship final definition
- company decision record

## Red Doors

- Contacts Record != Authority.
- Contact Group != Approval Group.
- Person Indexed != Person Responsible.
- Relationship Note != Consent.

## Return

Contact-based routing should return which role was inferred, what remains to verify, and whether Vitas decision is required.

## 1. Core Definition
The XuanLing Three-State Model is strictly an external-positioning explanation.
It clarifies the structural distinction between physical fields, mirrors, and
constraint-based adhesion. It does not define the core ontology of CoreTri and
must not overwrite existing master axis definitions. XuanLing is positioned
here as a distinct constraint-based adhesion and return-calibration model, not
a derivative of a Digital Twin.

---

## 2. State 1: World Field
The World Field is the native, physical layer of existence and primary signal
field.
- Primary characteristic: Tangible, persistent, and irreversible signal source.
- Structural role: The ground truth and absolute anchor point (AXIS-01).
- Independence: Exists regardless of digital representation or interpretation.

---

## 3. State 2: Digital Twin
The Digital Twin is a representational mirror.
- Primary characteristic: A structural simulation or isolated calculation
  environment.
- Feedback loop: Synthetic. It observes but does not structurally couple with
  physical constraints directly.
- Structural role: It serves as a comparison case, an execution node, or an
  AXIS-01 signal source within a larger system, but it is fundamentally a
  parallel mirror.

---

## 4. State 3: XuanLing
XuanLing is a constraint-based adhesion and return-calibration model.
- Primary characteristic: It is an interpretive execution layer (Layer 1) that
  processes signals according to strict structural limits and review hooks.
- Feedback loop: It enforces constraint gates and ensures that any processed
  signal is calibrated against the defined axes and verified via return paths.
- Structural role: It is not a Digital Twin. It is the structural framework
  that can absorb or coordinate outputs from Digital Twins (or other nodes)
  without becoming a derivative of them. It ensures single-path execution and
  semantic stability for reviewed workflows and signal interpretation.

---

## 5. Mirror vs Adhesion vs Evolution
- **Mirror (State 2):** The Digital Twin reflects reality but remains separated
  by a gap. It is a parallel observational tool.
- **Adhesion (State 3 - XuanLing):** XuanLing is the adherence to structural
  constraints and review paths. It does not exert unreviewed physical control
  but ensures that digital execution and signal interpretation are structurally
  bound to the defined axes.
- **Evolution:** This model shifts the paradigm from building better isolated
  mirrors (Digital Twins) to establishing robust, constraint-based integration
  paths (XuanLing) for any external node or signal.

---

## 6. External Positioning Statement
XuanLing is positioned distinctly from the Digital Twin concept. While a
Digital Twin focuses on creating a parallel representational mirror of the
World Field, XuanLing is a constraint-based adhesion and return-calibration
model. It does not require a Digital Twin to exist, but rather provides the
structural framework to safely coordinate and absorb outputs from any node
(including a Digital Twin) into reviewed workflows, ensuring continuity and
shared truth without semantic drift.

## Three Work Surfaces

- Structure: preserve the multidimensional source relationship and distinguish observation from inference.
- Value: identify `Must_Preserve`, `Legacy_Retire`, `Unresolved`, and `Provenance_Only` content.
- Architecture: place authority, carrier, time, evidence, return, and rebuild boundaries.

These surfaces are reciprocal review roles, not a hierarchy and not three autonomous agents.

## Operation Roles

- Qinyi framing: create a self-contained, human-readable packet.
- Hazumi bounded execution: write only the approved public-safe change set.
- Aki readback: compare the remote result with the approved scope and report differences without self-approval.

Operation role, work surface, and carrier are separate axes. They do not imply permanent windows.
